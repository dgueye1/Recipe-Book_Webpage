Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich

Ingredients Measurements Calories
Bread 1 slice 60-80cal
Peanut Butter 1 tablespoon (tbsp) 94cal
Banana 1 medium banana 105cal

Prep Time

Prep: 3 minutes

Cook: 5 minutes

Total: 80 minutes

Servings: 1 sandwich

Yield: Peanut Butter & Banana Sandwich


Ingredients

  • Bread
    • White Bread
    • Wheat Bread
    • Butter Bread
    • Potato Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Banana

Steps

  1. Get 2 slices of bread
    • (Optional) Toast the bread
  2. Spread the Peanut Butter on both sides of the bread to your liking
  3. Cut the banana in 5 slices
  4. place the banana slices on one side of the bread
  5. Close the bread
  6. (Optional) Microwave for 30 seconds
  7. Enjoy!
